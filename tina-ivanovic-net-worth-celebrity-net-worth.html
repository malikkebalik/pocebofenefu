What is Tina Ivanovic's Net Worth?

Tina Ivanovic is a Serbian singer and model who has a net worth of $12 million. Tina Ivanovic was born in Kosovska Kamenica, Yugoslavia in September 1974. Her styles include pop-folk and Balkan pop. Ivanovic released her debut studio album Lutalica in 1998.

She has also released the albums Zavodnica in 2000, Bunda od nerca in 2004, Extra in 2006, and Miris Ljubavi in 2008. Her first two albums were not a commercial success, but her single "Bunda od nerca" became an instant hit. Ivanovic's other singles include "Luda kuca", "Mali crni vrag", "Miluj me grubo", "Seksi" (with MC Stojan), "Kao magjia", "Ko manijak", "Lepoto moja", "Montenegro, Monte Carlo", "Ko rano poludi", "Vila u Brazilu", and "Vikend momak".
