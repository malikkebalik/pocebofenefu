What is Chase Elliott's Net Worth?

Chase Elliott is an American stock car racing driver who has a net worth of $12 million. Chase Elliott was born in Dawsonville, Georgia in November 1995. Chase is the son of 1988 Winston Cup Series champion Bill Elliott. Chase is a development driver for Hendrick Motorsports and currently drives full time in the NASCAR Nationwide Series where he drives the #9 NAPA Auto Parts Chevrolet Camaro for JR Motorsports. Elliot won the 2014 NASCAR Nationwide Series championship becoming the first rookie to win a NASCAR national series championship.

Elliott has also won the 2010 Winchester 400, 2011 Snowball Derby, 2012 World Crown 300, 2013 All American 400, and 2014 Nationwide Series Rookie of the Year. He has three NASCAR Nationwide Series wins, 26 top tens, and two poles. As a part of the NASCAR Camping World Truck Series he has one win, seven top tens, and one pole. In November 2014 he celebrated his Nationwide Series title that he wrapped up at the Phoenix International Raceway.
