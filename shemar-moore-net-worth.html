Shemar Moore Net Worth

Shemar Moore net worth:

$16 million

Shemar Moore is an American actor who also used to work as a fashion model. He began his acting career on television when he starred on the soap opera The Young and the Restless. He was on the show for several years. Simultaneously, he starred on the short-lived series Birds of Prey before attaining greater prominence for his role as Derek Morgan on Criminal Minds. He was part of the main cast until 2016 when his character left in Season 11. He also ventured into directing, making his debut in the movie The Bounce Back while also starring in it.
