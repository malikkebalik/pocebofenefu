John Deere 317 Garden Tractor is equipped with a Kolher KT17QS gasoline 2-cylinder air-cooled horizontal-shaft, 691 cc engine, which produces 17 hp power at rated rpm 3600.

The early versions suffered from insufficient lubrication of the connecting rod journals when run on hillsides.

An improved KT17 Series II engine was used beginning with engine specification number 24300 in 1982.

John Deere 317 Garden tractor is capable of using plows, tillers, or other ground-engaging attachments.
