What is Jaz-O's Net Worth?

Jaz-O is an American rapper and record producer who has a net worth of $500 thousand. Jaz-O was born in Brooklyn, New York in October 1964. He was the mentor of Jay-Z. Jaz-O  released his debut studio album Word to the Jaz in 1989. He also released the albums To Your Soul in 1990 and Kingz Kounty (with the Immobilarie) in 2002. Jaz-O released the EP Ya Don't Stop in 1991.

His single "The Originators" with Jay-Z reached #13 on the Hot Rap Songs chart. Jaz-O has had other singles on the Hot Rap Songs Top 20 including "Hawaiian Sophie" with Jay-Z, "A Groove (This Is What U Rap 2)", and "Love Is Gone". His single "Jigga What.." with Jay-Z and Amil reached #84 on the Hot 100. His single "Buss The Speaker" reached #26 on the Dance Club Songs chart. Jaz-O has also produced songs for artists including Group Home, Ras Kass, M.O.P, Kool G Rap, Rakim, and more.
