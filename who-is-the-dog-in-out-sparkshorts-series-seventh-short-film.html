Who is The Dog in Out SparkShorts Series Seventh Short Film?

The 2020 American computer-animated short film “Out” was released by Pixar Animation Studios and Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures. It is the seventh short film in the SparkShorts series.

The short was shortlisted for the Academy Award for Best Animated Short Film at the 93rd Academy Awards. Some viewers don’t know the name of the dog who appeared in “Out”, you will know the answer here.

What is the Name of the Dog in “Out”?

The question of what the dog’s name is in the SparkShort “Out” is a part of the Disney 100 TikTok quiz. Being able to answer this question is hard because not everyone watches SparkShorts.

What is the Story of “Out”?

In the story, a young gay man who hasn’t told his parents about his s*xual orientation experiences a sudden and miraculous mind-swap with his dog. Greg and his lover Manuel are packing up Greg’s things to relocate when a supernatural dog and cat arrive outside Greg’s townhouse.

The cat gives magic to Greg’s dog Jim’s collar. Inside, recalling a picture of them together, Manuel pushes Greg to tell his parents about his s*xual orientation and they rush to his aid without warning. Manuel slips out the back door and Greg quickly hides the photo.

With his hands on Jim’s collar, Greg mutters a wish to turn into a dog and their brains magically exchange bodies. While his calm father lights the grill, “Greg” runs outside to play in the yard. “Jim” is trying to get to him while also desperately trying to stop his mother from finding the picture.

She sits and talks out loud about how sad she is that her son is moving away while “Jim” listens. She is mad at the dog for acting up. If she could talk to Greg, she would tell him that she hopes he finds love and that “he makes you happy.”

“Jim” realizes all of a sudden that she knows and doesn’t mind that he is gay. He comforts her for a short time, then goes after “Greg” and changes their minds again. That night, Greg presents Manuel to his parents, and Greg’s dad gives Manuel a hug out of the blue. When the magical cat and dog finish their job, they jump off a rainbow.
