John Deere 40 HP Tractor Price, Specs, Review, Pictures 2022

John Deere 40 Price, Reviews, Specs, Engine Oil Capacity, Weight, Serial Numbers, Attachments, History, Features & Pictures

The John Deere 40 model was the replacement for the John Deere M. The 40 variants included the John Deere 40S utility, standard, high-crop, 2-row utility, and a crawler model (the 40C).

The John Deere 40C and the utility tractor were used as industrial models, although there was no official John Deere designation for 40 industrial models.

John Deere 40 Utility Tractor featured with a John Deere 1.6L 2-cyl gasoline and John Deere 1.6L 2-cyl all-fuel engine, 2900 to 4553 pounds weight, 4×2 2WD and 4 forward and 1 reverse gears.
