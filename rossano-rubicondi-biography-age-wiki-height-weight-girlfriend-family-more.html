Moving towards his career, Rossano took the first step towards his career as a model when he moved to London at the age of 22. Later, he started to cover small roles in the films such as “The Eighteenth Angel” and “The Golden Bowl”.

Furthermore, in the year 2008, he was selected as a contestant for the sixth edition of the Italian reality show “L’isola dei Stelle” on Rai 2. However, Rubicondi got eliminated during the eighth episode with 62% of the votes. Other than this, he hosted the TV Program “Cupido” in the year 2009.

In the same year, this handsome actor starred in the film Natale a Beverly Hills, playing a role that represents a parody of himself. Besides this, in the year 2010, he was asked to be a correspondent for the seventh edition of “L’isola dei Stelle”. Thereafter, he was also asked for the event of Rete 4 Parade of love and fashion in the year 2011.

Apart from that, Rossano returned as a competitor to the ninth edition of “The Island of the Famous” in the year 2012. However, he withdrew during the second episode for personal reasons. In addition to this, he was also a crucial part of the Italian entertainment show ‘Quelli che… il Calcio,’ from 2008 to 2010. Later, he was seen in the drama film ‘Head Full of Honey’ in the year 2018.
