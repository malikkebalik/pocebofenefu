Wayne Rooney and his wife have multiple properties around the globe. Including a secret GBP 5 million Barbados pad, GBP 300k Florida Apartment, GBP 6 million home in Prestbury, Cheshire, beautiful home in Maryland, and New GBP 20 million Cheshire Mansion.

The second property is a GBP 300k Florida Apartment. The couple bought this stunning waterfront apartment at Harbour Pointe in Port Charlotte, Florida, back in 2007.
