Tony Cavalero Net Worth

Tony Cavalero:

$1.5 million

Tony Cavalero is an American actor. He first appeared in an episode of Their Will Be Brawl in 2009. His first main role on TV was on Son of a Pitch in 2011. He next starred in the TV film Naughty or Nice. He next played recurring character Stanley Watts on Hart of Dixie. Afterward, he landed the role of Dewey Finn on the Nickelodeon series School of Rock, which aired from 2016 to 2018. In the movies, he got his first credited role in 2016’s After the Reality. In 2018, he appeared in the films Dog Days and When We First Met. This year, he portrayed Ozzy Osbourne in the Netflix movie The Dirt.
