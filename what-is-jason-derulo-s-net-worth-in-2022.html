What Is Jason Derulos Net Worth in 2022?

Jason Derulo is an award-winning R&B artist best known for songs “Savage Love,” “Whatcha Say,” and “Talk Dirty,” in addition to his hilarious presence on TikTok. What is this musician’s net worth in 2022? Here’s what we know about Derulo and how he makes his money. 

Jason Derulo released songs like ‘Savage Love,’ ‘Take You Dancing,’ and ‘Love Not War’

The R&B artist first rose to fame with songs like “In My Head” and “Watcha Say.” Since his appearance on the music scene, Derulo partnered with artists like Luke Bryan and Pentatonix to create original music. 

He even earned the CMT Music Award for CMT Performance of the Year for “Want to Want Me.” His song “Savage Love” also went viral on social media, with some fans creating dance moves to accompany the 2020 release.

Recently, Derulo’s popularity grew thanks to his social media presence, particularly on the video creation platform TikTok. During an interview with Complex, the “Talk Dirty” singer revealed that he makes about $75 thousand per Tiktok video, some of which even went viral on the app.

“I’m able to show who I am, whereas with other apps I couldn’t show me,” this artist shared. “TikTok is all about creation, and I love just building things from nothing. I love writing songs, I love screenwriting, I love making fun of myself.”

What is Jason Derulo’s net worth in 2022?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, as of 2022, Derulo’s net worth is an estimated $16 million. This is a culmination of income he earned from songwriting and producing for artists like Diddy, Danity Kane, Donnie Klang, Sean Kingston, Casses, and Lil Wayne in his early career as well as his original music.

Derulo also earns money from merchandise sales, live performances, and his appearances on television shows. That includes his roles on Lethal Weapons, So You Think You Can Dance, and Everybody Dance Now along with Destiny’s Child member Kelly Rowland. 

In 2019, he had a starring role in the movie musical adaptation of Cats. Of course, Derulo earned money (and followers) on social media platforms like TikTok and Instagram in recent years. 

Jason Derulo claims that his net worth is different than the amount stated online

While some fans wonder if Jason Derulo is a billionaire, the artist offered insight into his net worth, particularly sharing that the estimated total on Google is far off from the actual amount. 

“It’s so wrong,” Derulo told Complex. “I was actually talking to Forbes two days ago, and I was like, ‘Who comes up with the numbers? How do I get it fixed because the number is not even in the ballpark?’”

The artist also uses his platform to support his philanthropic work. Derulo created the Just For You Foundation, which raises funds “to promote education, providing shelter to orphans and families, feeding the homeless, and promoting health in the United States and globally.”
