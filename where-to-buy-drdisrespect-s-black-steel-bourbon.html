Where to buy DrDisRespects Black Steel Bourbon

Considered to be one of Twitch’s greatest mysteries after his banishment from the streaming platform in 2020, DrDisRespect never left the hustle life, not even after his sudden vanishing.

Recommended Videos

Guy Beahm, known by his online alias DrDisRespect, rose to fame in 2010 when he started uploading videos of himself playing Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2. In 2015, Beahm joined Justin.tv – now known as Twitch, started his full-time career as a video game streamer, and thus, the journey that would make him known across the internet as DrDisRespect.

His career as a Twitch streamer was cut short in 2020, when the streaming platform banished the streamer from its library, for unexplained reasons. Beahm would go on to start streaming on YouTube under the same eccentric online persona and as of 2022, DrDisRespect’s channel has surpassed four million subscribers. Last year, Beahm expanded his area of expertise into the world of alcohol by releasing his whiskey brand, Black Steel Bourbon.

Where can I buy Black Steel Bourbon?

The intoxicating mix of gaming and alcohol is a tale as old as time. So if you want to join in on the fun and try the Black Steel Bourbon, or even the Black Steel Bourbon 2 that was introduced earlier this year, we have some mixed news. The good news is, that you can easily find the beverage on the Black Steel Spirits website. To access it, you must be at least 21 years old, but keep in mind that it can only be shipped to the following locations:

  • Delaware
  • Florida
  • Maine
  • Maryland
  • New Jersey
  • New York
  • North Carolina
  • South Carolina

On the other hand, the bad news is that both Batch 1 and Batch 2 — which were limited editions — are completely sold out for the time being. There haven’t been any announcements regarding the restocking of the beverages, however, you can still sign up to receive Black Steel’s newsletter and notifications regarding any stock updates.

There you go. For now, all you can do is patiently wait for a new batch to finally be available. In the meantime, you can still keep up with Beahm on his YouTube channel, though with the unfortunate knowledge that you won’t be accompanied by an icy glass of Black Steel Bourbon.
