What is Paul Scholes' Net Worth?

Paul Scholes is an English retired professional soccer player who has a net worth of $25 million. Paul Scholes was born in Salford, England in 1974 and grew up in Langley where he played both cricket and soccer in school. Paul Scholes played his entire career for Manchester United, starting with their youth league at the age of 14, signing as an amateur in 1991 after leaving school, and then turning pro in 1993. He made his professional debut with Manchester United during the 1994-1995 season. Paul Scholes is considered by his fellow soccer players to be one of the best midfielders of his generation. During his career with Manchester United, the team won 11 Premier League titles, three FA Cup titles, and two UEFA Champions League titles.

Paul Scholes played on the England National Team from 1997-2004, playing in both the 1998 and 2002 World Cup Tournaments. Paul Scholes played in over 700 games for Manchester United. In 1999, Paul Scholes married his childhood sweetheart Claire. The couple has three children. Paul Scholes retired from his 19 year professional soccer career in May 2013.
